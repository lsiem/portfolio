## Phase Goal

**As a** visitor to lsiem.de, **I want to** move through the portfolio as a distinctive, engineered scroll-storytelling journey with felt craft, **so that** I remember Lasse as an engineer who builds and operates systems with taste — while never losing the one-click fast path to the facts.

*(This plan completes that journey: the signature photo treatment and case-study/about consistency, then verifies the whole immersive experience — desktop wow, reduced-motion quiet variant, and deliberate mobile — end to end.)*

<objective>
Extend the engineered identity to the last surfaces and prove the phase's promises hold everywhere. Design the signature photo treatment for the About section (D-16: 1:1 grid frame + L-shaped accent corner ticks + restrained `grayscale/contrast` duotone), with the actual image still owner-supplied and the section degrading to text-only. Bring the case-study (D-15) and prose/about pages up to the engineered-but-reading-first art direction (Bricolage display H1 via `SplitHeading`, `Reveal`-wrapped body). Then run the full-phase verification checkpoint: desktop immersion, the complete reduced-motion quiet variant (MODE-02), deliberate mobile with no scrolljacking (TECH-02), and the CI CWV/bundle gate — on both locales.

Purpose: Deliver the deferred signature photo treatment and cross-site consistency, and close the phase with a human-verified walkthrough of the three success dimensions (wow / quiet / mobile).
Output: photo-treatment CSS + slot, elevated case-study + prose pages, final eval contracts, and the phase verification checkpoint.
</objective>
