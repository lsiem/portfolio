## Phase Goal

**As a** visitor to lsiem.de, **I want to** move through the portfolio as a distinctive, engineered scroll-storytelling journey with felt craft, **so that** I remember Lasse as an engineer who builds and operates systems with taste — while never losing the one-click fast path to the facts.

*(This plan delivers the storytelling core of that journey: the career as scroll-linked scenes with the ITSC arc as centerpiece, plus the flagship bento project hierarchy.)*

<objective>
Turn the career and projects sections into the phase's storytelling centerpiece on the primitives built in plan 01. Add the `CareerSpine` (D-07 progress rail + coordinate ticks) and apply progressive reveal-on-enter to each career chapter with the ITSC role arc expanded into an emphasized multi-beat sub-sequence (D-05, D-06). Add the `ProjectBento` (D-14 featured hierarchy — ELIA + Vidama large, the rest compact) on the widened structural grid (D-04). Content stays server-rendered and fully present under reduced-motion, touch, and below `lg` (WOW-04, MODE-02, TECH-02); native scroll only, no pinning, no scrolljacking (D-05).

Purpose: Satisfy WOW-02 (scroll-verknüpfte Szenen pro Karriere-Kapitel) and deliver the deferred heavier flagship-project treatment, reusing plan 01's Reveal/matchMedia/token infrastructure rather than inventing new motion.
Output: `CareerSpine`, `ProjectBento`, the elevated career + projects sections, and career/bento eval contracts.
</objective>
