## Phase Goal

**As a** visitor to lsiem.de, **I want to** move through the portfolio as a distinctive, engineered scroll-storytelling journey with felt craft, **so that** I remember Lasse as an engineer who builds and operates systems with taste — while never losing the one-click fast path to the facts.

*(This plan delivers the "everything feels designed" layer of that journey: micro-interactions and seamless section/route transitions — the felt-craft half of WOW-03.)*

<objective>
Layer the craft micro-interactions and seamless transitions on top of the storytelling built in plans 01-02, on the single GSAP engine. Add the `Magnetic` wrapper (D-11.1, pointer-only via `contextSafe`, absent on touch), the designed hover/focus/active states across nav links, org/project title links, tech/skill chips, and the CV button (D-11.3), and the `TransitionLink` GSAP crossfade for sub-route navigation to case studies and `/about` (D-11.4). Preserve the global `:focus-visible` accent ring (TECH-03) and the accent scarcity budget (D-02). No second animation engine — the crossfade is GSAP, not the View Transitions API (D-08).

Purpose: Complete the WOW-03 micro-interaction + seamless-transition surface while keeping mobile deliberate (magnetic absent on touch, TECH-02) and reduced-motion quiet (CSS motion gated).
Output: `Magnetic`, `TransitionLink`, the designed interaction-state CSS, and their eval contracts.
</objective>
