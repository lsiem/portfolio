## Problem & Context

The sales partners of EWE and osnatel needed centrally managed marketing
media with regional control: campaign films had to be managed, assigned to
the right partners, and played back directly in the shop — without every
location maintaining its own content by hand.

## Solution

The result is an enterprise web application with a consistent role model for
administrators, sales partners, and delegates. The media library combines
media management and shop integration in a single interface: campaign films
are uploaded centrally, assigned regionally, and played at the point of sale.

Template-based content creation lets new content be derived from existing
templates instead of being produced from scratch for every campaign — keeping
maintenance manageable even across many partners and regions.

## Role

As CTO of Vidama GmbH I was the sole developer, responsible for all custom
software development — from planning through implementation to maintenance.
The application runs as a containerized Java/Spring Boot application with a
Vaadin frontend and PostgreSQL, delivered through a CI/CD pipeline.
